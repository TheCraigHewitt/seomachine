# Email Types Reference

## Onboarding Emails

### New Users Series
- **Trigger**: New signup (free/trial)
- **Goal**: Reach activation quickly
- **Typical sequence**: 5-7 emails over ~14 days
- **Flow**: Welcome -> quick win -> feature highlight -> proof -> check-in -> advanced tip -> next milestone

### New Customers Series
- **Trigger**: First payment
- **Goal**: Reinforce purchase and reduce early churn
- **Typical sequence**: 3-5 emails over ~14 days
- **Flow**: Thank you -> setup checklist -> paid feature value -> similar-customer proof -> support check-in

### Key Onboarding Step Reminder
- **Trigger**: Critical step incomplete after X hours/days
- **Goal**: Complete high-value action
- **Format**: 1-3 emails
- **Copy pattern**: remind progress -> why this step matters -> direct completion link -> help option

### New User Invite
- **Trigger**: Teammate invite sent
- **Goal**: Activate invited user
- **Typical sequence**: 2-3 emails
- **Copy pattern**: inviter context -> what they are joining -> single accept CTA

## Retention Emails

### Upgrade to Paid
- **Trigger**: Trial end, high engagement, or usage limit
- **Goal**: Free/trial -> paid conversion
- **Typical sequence**: 3-5 emails
- **Flow**: value summary -> what is locked -> proof -> urgency -> final offer

### Upgrade to Higher Plan
- **Trigger**: Approaching limits or advanced behavior
- **Goal**: Expansion
- **Format**: 1-3 emails
- **Copy pattern**: growth acknowledgment -> unlocked value -> easy upgrade path

### Ask for Review
- **Trigger**: Positive milestone, renewal, successful support interaction
- **Goal**: New public reviews
- **Format**: Single email
- **Copy pattern**: thank-you -> specific value -> why review matters -> direct review link

### Offer Support Proactively
- **Trigger**: Usage drop, repeated errors, stalled onboarding
- **Goal**: Save at-risk users
- **Format**: Single email
- **Copy pattern**: specific observation -> help offer -> fast resolution path

### Product Usage Report
- **Trigger**: Weekly/monthly schedule
- **Goal**: Demonstrate delivered value
- **Format**: Recurring single email
- **Include**: activity summary, trend vs previous period, wins, recommended next action

### NPS Survey
- **Trigger**: Time-based or post-milestone
- **Goal**: Satisfaction signal + follow-up routing
- **Format**: Single email
- **Follow-up**:
  - Promoters (9-10): ask for referral/review
  - Passives (7-8): ask what would make it a 10
  - Detractors (0-6): direct support outreach

### Referral Program
- **Trigger**: Milestone, promoter score, renewal
- **Goal**: New referred users
- **Format**: Single email or periodic reminders
- **Copy pattern**: success reminder -> reward clarity -> one-click sharing

## Billing Emails

### Switch to Annual
- **Trigger**: Monthly renewal cycles or campaign windows
- **Goal**: Improve retention and cash flow
- **Format**: 1-2 emails
- **Include**: explicit savings, lock-in option, one-click switch

### Failed Payment Recovery
- **Trigger**: Payment failure
- **Goal**: Recover revenue before churn
- **Typical sequence**: 3-4 emails over 7-14 days
- **Flow**: friendly notice -> reminder -> urgent warning -> final notice

### Cancellation Survey
- **Trigger**: Subscription cancellation
- **Goal**: Learn reason and save account when possible
- **Format**: Single immediate email (or in-app first)
- **Include**: reason capture, optional save offer path

### Upcoming Renewal Reminder
- **Trigger**: 14-30 days pre-renewal
- **Goal**: Avoid surprise charge and reduce disputes/churn
- **Format**: Single email
- **Include**: date, amount, included value, plan/payment update links

## Usage Emails

### Daily/Weekly/Monthly Summary
- **Trigger**: Time-based
- **Goal**: Sustain engagement
- **Format**: Recurring single email
- **Structure**: key metrics -> notable change -> next best action

### Key Event or Milestone Notification
- **Trigger**: Notable achievement
- **Goal**: Reinforce progress and momentum
- **Format**: Single email
- **Examples**: first key action, streaks, volume milestones

## Win-Back Emails

### Expired Trials
- **Trigger**: Trial expiry without conversion
- **Goal**: Recover qualified trials
- **Typical sequence**: 3-4 emails over ~30 days
- **Flow**: expiry reminder -> friction question -> incentive -> final reopen

### Cancelled Customers
- **Trigger**: 30/60/90 days after churn
- **Goal**: Reactivate when blockers are resolved
- **Typical sequence**: 2-3 emails over ~90 days
- **Flow**: what changed -> issue addressed -> return offer

## Campaign Emails

### Monthly Roundup / Newsletter
- **Trigger**: Monthly cadence
- **Goal**: Ongoing engagement and recall
- **Format**: Single recurring email
- **Mix**: product updates, stories, educational content, key links

### Seasonal Promotions
- **Trigger**: Calendar moments (BFCM, New Year, etc.)
- **Goal**: Timed conversion lift
- **Format**: 2-4 email burst
- **Flow**: announce -> midpoint reminder -> final hours

### Product Updates
- **Trigger**: Feature release
- **Goal**: Adoption and momentum
- **Format**: Single email per major release
- **Include**: what changed -> why it matters -> how to use now

### Industry News Roundup
- **Trigger**: Weekly or monthly
- **Goal**: Thought leadership and repeat opens
- **Format**: Curated digest
- **Include**: links + commentary + practical implications

### Pricing Update
- **Trigger**: Pricing change
- **Goal**: Transparent communication and retention
- **Format**: Single email or short sequence
- **Typical timeline**: 30-60 day notice, 14 day reminder, final reminder
