# Page CRO Experiment Ideas

Focused A/B and multivariate test backlog by page type. Define one primary metric and one guardrail metric for each test.

## Homepage Experiments

### Hero and Messaging

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Outcome headline vs. product-descriptor headline | Visitors convert more when promise is specific and result-oriented | CTA click-through |
| Subheadline with quantified claim vs. generic copy | Concrete numbers reduce uncertainty | Conversion rate |
| Static screenshot vs. short product loop | Product-in-context visual improves comprehension | Scroll depth to CTA |
| Single CTA vs. primary + secondary CTA | Simplifying choices increases primary intent | Primary CTA clicks |
| Customer logo strip in hero vs. below fold | Early trust proof improves first-session conversion | Conversion rate |

### Information Architecture and UX

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Sticky nav CTA vs. no sticky CTA | Persistent access to action improves conversion | CTA clicks/session |
| Section order: benefits before features vs. features before benefits | Benefit-first order improves progression | Section-to-section progression |
| Long page vs. shorter summary version | Audience intent determines optimal argument length | Conversion rate |
| Live chat entry point vs. no chat entry point | Fast objection resolution increases qualified actions | Demo/contact starts |

## Pricing Page Experiments

### Price and Plan Presentation

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Monthly default vs. annual default with savings callout | Savings framing lifts annual selection | Annual plan share |
| 2-tier vs. 3-tier plan table | Reduced cognitive load improves plan selection | Plan-select clicks |
| "Most Popular" marker on target plan vs. none | Guided choice increases target-plan starts | Target plan CTR |
| Feature table with tooltips vs. compact bullets | Better clarity lowers indecision | Checkout starts |
| ROI snippet near pricing vs. no ROI snippet | Value framing reduces price resistance | Paid conversion rate |

### Objection and Risk Reduction

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| FAQ block directly under pricing vs. separate tab | Immediate objection handling improves progression | Checkout starts |
| Trial/no-credit-card message prominence | Lower perceived risk improves first commitment | Trial starts |
| Testimonials focused on ROI vs. generic praise | Specific outcomes increase trust in price-value fit | Paid conversion |

## Demo Request Page Experiments

### Form Strategy

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| 4-field form vs. 7-field form | Lower effort increases completion | Form completion rate |
| Single-step vs. two-step form with progress | Staged commitment can reduce abandonment | Completion rate |
| Calendar embed inline vs. post-submit scheduling | Immediate scheduling increases booked demos | Booked demo rate |
| Phone required vs. optional with explanation | Optional phone improves completion without lowering quality | Completion + SQL rate |
| Work email validation strict vs. soft | Strict filtering changes lead quality mix | SQL acceptance rate |

### Proof and Expectation Setting

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| "What you'll get" checklist above form vs. below | Clear outcomes improve willingness to submit | Form start rate |
| Case study snippet near CTA vs. no snippet | Concrete proof improves completion | Completion rate |
| Response-time promise ("within 24h") vs. none | Certainty about next step reduces hesitation | Submit rate |

## Landing Page Experiments

### Message Match and Focus

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Ad-copy-matched headline vs. generalized headline | Tight message match reduces bounce and boosts conversion | Conversion rate |
| Navigation removed vs. full site nav | Fewer exits increase focused action | Primary conversion |
| One CTA repeated vs. mixed CTA intents | Singular intent improves completion | Primary CTA clicks |
| Short version vs. long-form argument | Offer complexity determines needed depth | Conversion rate |
| Hero proof element (logos or stat) vs. none | Early credibility improves high-intent completion | Form/CTA completion |

## Feature and Content Page Experiments

### Feature Pages

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Feature screenshot with annotation vs. text-only | Tangible demonstration increases intent | Trial/demo clicks |
| Use-case blocks by persona vs. single generic block | Relevance improves conversion by segment | Segment conversion rate |
| Feature-specific CTA vs. generic global CTA | CTA-context match increases action | CTA conversion |
| Before/after workflow visualization vs. bullet list | Process clarity lowers perceived complexity | Demo starts |

### Blog/Resource Pages

| Test | Hypothesis | Primary Metric |
|------|------------|----------------|
| Inline CTA after first value section vs. end-only CTA | Mid-article CTA captures engaged readers | CTA clicks |
| Topic-matched lead magnet vs. generic newsletter CTA | Relevance improves opt-in rate | Email/signup conversion |
| Sticky side CTA vs. inline only | Persistent access improves conversion on long content | Conversion per session |
| End-of-post "next best action" module vs. none | Guided progression lifts downstream conversion | Assisted conversions |
