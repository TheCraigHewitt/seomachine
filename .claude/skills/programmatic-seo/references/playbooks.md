# The 12 Programmatic SEO Playbooks

Proven playbooks for building pages at scale.

## Playbook Matrix

| # | Playbook | Pattern | Why It Works | Value Requirements | URL Structure |
|---|----------|---------|-------------|-------------------|---------------|
| 1 | Templates | "[type] template" | High intent, shareable assets | Actually usable, multiple variations, quality matches paid | `/templates/[type]/` |
| 2 | Curation | "best [category]" | Comparison shoppers, high commercial intent | Genuine criteria, real testing, regular updates | `/best/[category]/` |
| 3 | Conversions | "[X] to [Y]" | Instant utility, massive volume | Accurate real-time data, fast tool, mobile-friendly | `/convert/[from]-to-[to]/` |
| 4 | Comparisons | "[X] vs [Y]" | High purchase intent, clear pattern | Honest analysis, real feature data, clear recs | `/compare/[x]-vs-[y]/` |
| 5 | Examples | "[type] examples" | Research phase, highly shareable | Real high-quality examples, screenshots, analysis | `/examples/[type]/` |
| 6 | Locations | "[service] in [location]" | Massive local intent, scales with geography | Actual local data (not city-name swaps), local insights | `/[service]/[city]/` |
| 7 | Personas | "[product] for [audience]" | Speaks to searcher's context, higher conversion | Genuine persona-specific content, relevant testimonials | `/for/[persona]/` |
| 8 | Integrations | "[product A] [product B]" | Captures partner product users | Real integration details, setup instructions, use cases | `/integrations/[product]/` |
| 9 | Glossary | "what is [term]" | Top-of-funnel awareness, expertise signals | Clear definitions, examples, related terms linked | `/glossary/[term]/` |
| 10 | Translations | Same content, other languages | Opens new markets, lower competition | Quality translation, cultural localization, hreflang tags | `/[lang]/[page]/` |
| 11 | Directory | "[category] tools" | Research phase capture, link magnet | Comprehensive coverage, useful filtering, details per listing | `/directory/[category]/` |
| 12 | Profiles | "[entity name]" | Informational traffic, topical authority | Accurate sourced info, regularly updated, unique insights | `/people/[name]/` |

---

## Choosing Your Playbook

| If you have... | Consider... |
|----------------|-------------|
| Proprietary data | Directories, Profiles |
| Product with integrations | Integrations |
| Design/creative product | Templates, Examples |
| Multi-segment audience | Personas |
| Local presence | Locations |
| Tool or utility product | Conversions |
| Content/expertise | Glossary, Curation |
| International potential | Translations |
| Competitor landscape | Comparisons |

### Combining Playbooks

Layer multiple playbooks for compound targeting:
- **Locations + Personas**: "Marketing agencies for startups in Austin"
- **Curation + Locations**: "Best coworking spaces in San Diego"
- **Integrations + Personas**: "Slack for sales teams"
- **Glossary + Translations**: Multi-language educational content

*See also: competitor-alternatives skill for detailed comparison page frameworks.*
