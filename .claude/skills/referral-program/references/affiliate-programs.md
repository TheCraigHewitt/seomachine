# Affiliate Program Design

## Commission Structures

- **Percentage of sale**: Typical 10-30% (first purchase or first year)
- **Flat fee per action**: Typical $5-500 per qualified action
- **Recurring commission**: Typical 10-25% recurring rev share
- **Tiered commission**: higher % for higher monthly volume

## Cookie Duration

| Duration | Typical Use Case |
|----------|------------------|
| 24 hours | Low-consideration impulse purchases |
| 7-14 days | Standard e-commerce |
| 30 days | Common SaaS/B2B baseline |
| 60-90 days | Longer evaluation cycles |
| Lifetime | Strategic partner agreements |

## Affiliate Recruitment

### Sources
- Existing customers with audience reach
- Niche bloggers and newsletter operators
- YouTubers and educators
- Agencies/consultants serving your ICP
- Complementary products

### Outreach Template

```text
Subject: Partnership opportunity - [Your Product]

Hi [Name],

I follow your work on [topic], especially [specific piece].
[Your Product] helps [audience] achieve [outcome], and there is clear fit with your audience.

We offer [commission structure] plus [extra partner benefit].
Would you like details?

[Your name]
```

## Affiliate Enablement Checklist

- [ ] Unique tracking links/codes
- [ ] Product and audience summary
- [ ] Key differentiators vs alternatives
- [ ] Creative assets and sample copy
- [ ] Proof assets (case studies/testimonials)
- [ ] FAQ + objection handling
- [ ] Payment terms and schedule

## Tools

### Referral Program Tools

### Affiliate Program Tools
- ShareASale, Impact, PartnerStack, Tapfiliate, FirstPromoter, Rewardful, Refersion

### Tool Selection Criteria
- Payment stack integration
- Fraud controls
- Payout handling
- Reporting clarity
- Customization needs

## Fraud Prevention

### Common Abuse Patterns
- Self-referrals
- Referral rings
- Coupon-site abuse
- Fake identity/account farms

### Prevention Controls
- Email/account verification
- Device/IP pattern checks
- Delayed payout until activation milestone
- Reward caps and clawback terms
- Manual review for anomaly flags
