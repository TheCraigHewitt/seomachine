# AI Writing Detection

Checklist to reduce AI-sounding copy and improve readability.

## Primary Signals

1. Overuse of em dashes for parenthetical phrases.
2. Repetitive transitions and formulaic openers.
3. Inflated verbs/adjectives instead of plain words.
4. Generic conclusions that say little.
5. Filler intensifiers that add no meaning.

---

## Em Dash Rule

Use commas, colons, or parentheses by default. Reserve em dashes for rare emphasis.

| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|
| The results---which were surprising---improved conversion. | The results, which were surprising, improved conversion. |
| This approach---unlike legacy methods---cuts setup time. | This approach, unlike legacy methods, cuts setup time. |

## Structural Patterns to Avoid

- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "In the ever-evolving landscape of..."
- "At its core..."
- "This begs the question..."
- "In conclusion..."

---

## Self-Check Routine

1. Read aloud and mark unnatural lines.
2. Replace inflated words with plain alternatives.
3. Cut filler intensifiers (`very`, `really`, `extremely`).
4. Break repeated sentence structures.
5. Verify each claim is specific and supported.
