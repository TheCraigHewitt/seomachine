# Social Content

Read `.claude/product-marketing-context.md` if it exists.

## Before Creating Content

Capture:
1. **Goal**: awareness, engagement, leads, sales, community.
2. **Audience**: who, where they engage, what they share.
3. **Voice**: tone, boundaries, brand terms.
4. **Resources**: weekly time, existing assets, video capacity.

## Platform Quick Reference

| Platform | Best For | Typical Frequency | Key Format |
|----------|----------|-------------------|------------|
| LinkedIn | B2B authority | 3-5 posts/week | Story posts, carousels |
| Twitter/X | Real-time conversation | 3-10 posts/day incl. replies | Threads, commentary |
| Instagram | Visual education/brand | 1-2 feed posts/day + Stories | Reels, carousels |
| TikTok | Top-of-funnel reach | 1-4 posts/day | Short native video |
| Facebook | Community and groups | 1-2 posts/day | Groups, native video |

Detailed platform tactics: [references/platforms.md](references/platforms.md)

## Content Pillars Framework

| Pillar | % of Content | Example Topics |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| Industry insights | 30% | Trends, data, predictions |
| Behind-the-scenes | 25% | Building process, lessons |
| Educational | 25% | How-tos, frameworks |
| Personal perspective | 15% | Stories, opinion |
| Promotional | 5% | Product updates, offers |

## Hook Formulas

### Curiosity Hooks
- "I was wrong about [common belief]."
- "The real reason [outcome] happens is not what you think."
- "[Result] in [timeframe], here is what changed."

### Story Hooks
- "Last week, [unexpected thing] happened."
- "I almost [mistake/failure]."
- "[Time ago], I was [past state]. Today: [current state]."

### Value Hooks
- "How to [outcome] without [pain]:"
- "[Number] ways to [benefit]:"
- "Stop [mistake]. Do this instead:"

### Contrarian Hooks
- "Unpopular opinion: [statement]"
- "[Common advice] is wrong. Here is why:"
- "I stopped [practice] and got [result]."

## Content Repurposing System

### Blog Post -> Social Adaptations

| Platform | Repurpose Format |
|----------|------------------|
| LinkedIn | Insight post + comments CTA |
| LinkedIn | Carousel summary |
| Twitter/X | Thread with key takeaways |
| Instagram | Carousel + caption summary |
| Instagram/TikTok | 30-second reel recap |

### Repurposing Workflow
1. Create one pillar asset (article/video/podcast).
2. Extract 3-5 sharp points.
3. Reframe by platform context.
4. Schedule across the week.
5. Repost winners with fresh hooks.

## Content Calendar Structure

### Weekly Template

| Day | LinkedIn | Twitter/X | Instagram |
|-----|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Mon | Insight | Thread | Carousel |
| Tue | BTS post | Replies + short takes | Stories |
| Wed | How-to | Tips thread | Reel |
| Thu | Story post | Breakdown thread | Educational carousel |
| Fri | Contrarian take | Engagement prompts | Stories |

### Batching Strategy (2-3 hrs/week)
1. Select pillar topics.
2. Draft core posts.
3. Prepare visual assets.
4. Schedule queue.
5. Leave slots for live posts.

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Routine (30 minutes)
1. Reply to all inbound comments.
2. Leave 5-10 high-quality comments on target accounts.
3. Re-share one relevant post with added perspective.
4. Send 2-3 context-relevant DMs.

### Comment Quality Rules
- Add new information.
- Share real experience.
- Ask a useful follow-up.
- Disagree with evidence, not tone.

## Analytics & Optimization

### Metrics
- Awareness: impressions, reach, follower growth
- Engagement: comment/share/save rates
- Conversion: profile visits, link clicks, DMs, attributed leads

### Weekly Review
- Top 3 posts and why they worked
- Bottom 3 posts and fix hypotheses
- Format and hook performance by platform
- Time-slot performance

### Optimization Actions
- Low engagement -> test stronger hooks and formats.
- Declining reach -> increase native engagement and content consistency.
- Low conversion -> improve post-to-profile/landing transition.
