# Platform-Specific Strategy Guide

## LinkedIn

**Best for:** B2B authority, recruiting, partnership visibility  
**Typical frequency:** 3-5 posts/week

**What works**
- Personal stories with business takeaways
- Data-backed insights
- Carousels/document posts
- Comments that extend the conversation

**What underperforms**
- Generic motivation posts
- Link-heavy promotional posts
- Corporate jargon without POV

**Format notes**
- First line must carry the hook before "see more"
- Use line breaks for readability
- Typical: longer posts can perform when they keep dwell time

## Twitter/X

**Best for:** Fast commentary, niche communities, operator voice  
**Typical frequency:** 3-10 posts/day including replies

**What works**
- Strong opinions with evidence
- Threads with clear promise and delivery
- Quote-tweets with additional analysis
- Real-time participation in relevant conversations

**What underperforms**
- Pure self-promotion
- Threads without a hook
- Low reply activity

**Format notes**
- Short posts often get quick engagement
- Visuals can improve stop-rate
- First 30 minutes engagement often affects distribution

## Instagram

**Best for:** Visual education, brand affinity, creator-led storytelling  
**Typical frequency:** 1-2 feed posts/day + Stories

**What works**
- Reels with immediate hooks
- Educational carousels
- Story interactions (polls/questions)
- UGC and behind-the-scenes content

**What underperforms**
- Low-quality visuals
- Overly text-heavy assets
- Feed-only strategy with no Stories/Reels

**Format notes**
- Typical: short vertical video often gets broader distribution than static posts
- Saves and shares matter more than likes for long-tail reach

## TikTok

**Best for:** Top-of-funnel awareness and rapid format testing  
**Typical frequency:** 1-4 posts/day

**What works**
- Native-feeling short videos
- Trend adaptation with domain relevance
- Fast hooks in first 1-2 seconds
- Comment-to-video responses

**What underperforms**
- Overproduced ads disguised as organic
- Slow intros
- Hard-selling in first seconds

## Facebook

**Best for:** Communities, local businesses, older-skewing audiences  
**Typical frequency:** 1-2 posts/day

**What works**
- Group conversations
- Native video/live sessions
- Local or practical posts with discussion prompts

**What underperforms**
- Pure outbound links
- Cross-posted content without adaptation
- Low comment responsiveness
