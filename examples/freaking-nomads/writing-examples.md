## Key Formatting Patterns

**What every article has:**
- Hook in first sentence (gut-punch, personal story, or provocative question)
- Specific numbers within first 3 paragraphs
- H2 headings that answer real questions ("Does Vietnam have a digital nomad visa?")
- At least one comparison table or cost breakdown
- FAQ section at end
- Single CTA at bottom (join community / newsletter)

**What every article avoids:**
- Opening with "In today's world..." or "Digital nomadism is becoming..."
- Vague descriptors: "affordable," "fast internet," "vibrant community"
- Passive voice
- Buried cons
- "In conclusion..."
- Affiliate disclosures buried at the bottom (declared upfront if present)
